Questions & Answers

Am I eligible for a home loan?
+
If you're 18+ and earn a regular income (full-time, part-time, casual, or self-employed), you can apply. Whether you're buying your first home, upgrading, refinancing, or investing, we'll match you with lenders that suit your situation. The quickest way to know what you can borrow is to start our pre-check form: no obligation, no hard credit hit.
How long does the application take?
+
Most clients complete the online form in under 5 minutes. You'll be contacted by one of our finance specialists within one business hour of submission.
Will applying affect my credit score?
+
Our pre-check uses a soft inquiry, so nothing is recorded on your credit file. A hard inquiry only happens after you give the green light to proceed — protecting your score while you shop around.
What interest rate could I get?
+
Home loan rates currently start from 5.74% p.a.* and vary based on your deposit, credit profile, loan type (owner-occupier vs investment), and whether you choose fixed or variable. Complete the pre-check to see the personalised rates available to you — there's no cost to look.
Do you handle first home buyers, refinancers, and investors?
+
All three. We work with a nationwide panel of lenders who cover first home buyer loans (including First Home Guarantee applications), refinances, investment loans, construction loans, and self-employed borrowers. Tell us your situation in the form and we'll tailor the comparison.
My credit isn't perfect — should I still apply?
+
Absolutely. We specialise in matching every credit profile with the lender most likely to say yes. More than one-third of the approvals we secure each week are for clients the big banks declined.
How long until I'm approved and can settle?
+
Conditional approval is typically issued within 24–48 hours of your full application. Full approval and settlement timing depends on the lender and your property — most refinances settle within 2–4 weeks, and purchase loans align with your contract settlement date. We'll keep you updated every step of the way.
Are there any hidden fees?
+
No surprises. Any establishment or ongoing fees are disclosed upfront during the quote stage. You'll see the full cost summary before you commit.
Can I repay my loan early?
+
Most of our lender partners allow early or extra repayments without penalty. We'll always flag any exceptions before you sign.
What's the next step after reading this?
+
Click "Start Now" below. In less time than it takes to make a coffee, you'll know your eligibility, your estimated rate, and be on the road to securing the asset you want.
